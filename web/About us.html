What is INOVA



Inova allows users to browse and buy a wide range of games from various developers and publishers. Once a game is purchased, it is added to the user's library and can be downloaded and installed on their computer. Inova also provides features like automatic updates for games, community forums, social networking features, and multiplayer functionality.



Is is free to use?



Inova itself is free to download and use. You can create an Inova account without any cost and access various features, such as browsing the store, adding games to your wishlist, and participating in community forums. Additionally, you can use Steam's social features, such as chatting with friends, joining groups, and sharing game content.
